About VIT University

Discover our history, mission, and values

Our Story

VIT University (Vellore Institute of Technology) is one of India's premier institutions, established in 1984 by Dr. G. Viswanathan. From its humble beginnings, VIT has grown into a world-class university, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

VIT has been recognized for its excellence in education, research, and innovation. The university's infrastructure, state-of-the-art research facilities, and strong industry collaborations make it a top choice for students across India and internationally.

With a commitment to academic excellence, VIT follows a unique education model that blends technical knowledge with industry exposure, making students highly skilled and job-ready. The university also emphasizes ethics, leadership, and social responsibility.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

VIT University is dedicated to providing quality higher education with a global perspective. Our mission is to create a world-class learning environment that nurtures innovation, research, and skill development to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

VIT aims to be recognized as a global leader in education and research, producing graduates who contribute to the technological, social, and economic growth of society.
